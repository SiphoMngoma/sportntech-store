About SportNTech

Welcome to SportNTech, your premier destination for the latest in sports gear and technology. Founded in 2023, we've quickly become a trusted name for athletes and tech enthusiasts alike.

Our mission is to provide high-quality products that combine cutting-edge technology with sports performance. Whether you're looking for the newest smartphone to capture your adventures or the perfect pair of soccer cleats, we've got you covered.

At SportNTech, we believe in:

  • Quality products from trusted brands
  • Competitive pricing without compromise
  • Exceptional customer service
  • Fast and reliable delivery

Visit our store at Nomakhele Road, Highflats, or shop online anytime. Our team is always ready to help you find exactly what you need.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary depending on your location. For major cities in South Africa, we typically deliver within 2-3 business days. For rural areas, it may take up to 5 business days. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

Do you sell original products?

Yes, all our products are 100% authentic and come with manufacturer warranties. We source directly from authorized distributors and brands to ensure you receive genuine products.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers (EFT). We also offer cash on delivery for orders within Highflats.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes, we offer a 14-day return policy for unused products in their original packaging. For exchanges, please contact our customer service within 7 days of receiving your order.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number on our website or the courier's website to track your package in real-time.
